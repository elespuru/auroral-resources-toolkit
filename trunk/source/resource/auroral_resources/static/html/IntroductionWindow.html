Usage Notes

  • 1. Choose a time range to the left under 'Active Time Range'. It defaults to the past week.
  • 2. Expand resource topics in the folders to the left. To add components to your workspace, you drag them from the left folder area, to the gray center area of the page. Unless otherwise explicitly stated, all components are connected to the time control to the left, and will update when you make adjustments accordingly.

A note on cookies

In lieu of a user account, this site uses cookies to remember your decisions. No personal information is stored, solely things like your time range, active components etc. so that the next time you visit the site, it will look the same way it did when you left. If you're not comfortable with this, simply disable cookies for this site, using your browser's settings for doing so. If you choose to do so, we will not be able to restore your settings however, and you'll have to rebuild your workspace each time your visit.
