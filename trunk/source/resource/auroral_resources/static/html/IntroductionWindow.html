Quick Start

  • Choose a time range, and "now" to the left under 'Active Time Range'. It defaults to the past week.
  • To add components to your workspace, drag them from the left folder area to the gray center area.
  • Adjust the "now" time slider to select a specific time for imagery, and the red vertical epoch marker.
  • Right-clicking a widget/window will bring up a menu for data and metadata downloads.
  • All components are connected to the time control, please be patient when updating.
